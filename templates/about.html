BEING A SOLDIER

As a Soldier in the Indian Army, you're tasked with upholding the Constitution and protecting Indian's freedoms. But it's not always work — Soldiers have free time just like everyone else to spend with friends and family, or take part in sports or hobbies. Soldiers continually strengthen themselves mentally and physically through Ongoing Training. And most important — Soldiers live the Seven Core Army Values every daY.
